Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy cryptocurrencies?

To buy cryptocurrencies, first, click on buy, a form will pop up, fill it and submit.

How can I sell my cryptocurrencies?

Selling cryptocurrencies is quite easy, click on sell a form will pop up, fill it and click on submit.

What fees are associated with buying and selling?

We charge a small transaction fee for each buy and sell order. The exact fee depends on the trading volume and the cryptocurrency being traded.
