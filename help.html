Help & Support

Getting Started

To get started with Crypto Exchange, follow these steps:

  1. Login to ADE'S WALLET.
  2. Navigate to the 'Buy' or 'Sell' section to trade cryptocurrencies.

Buying Cryptocurrencies

To buy cryptocurrencies:

  • Log into ADE'S WALLET.
  • Go to the 'Buy' section.
  • Select the cryptocurrency you want to purchase and enter the amount.
  • Review the transaction details and confirm your purchase.

Selling Cryptocurrencies

To sell cryptocurrencies:

  • Log into ADE'S WALLET.
  • Navigate to the 'Sell' section.
  • Choose the cryptocurrency you wish to sell and specify the amount.
  • Review the sale details and confirm the transaction.
